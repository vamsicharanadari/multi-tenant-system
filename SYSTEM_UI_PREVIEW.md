### Features Demonstrated:
- **Authentication**: Unified portal for Super Admins and Tenant Users.
- **Control Plane**: Automated physical database-per-tenant provisioning and God-Mode impersonation.
- **Dynamic White-Labeling**: Automatic theme switching (`--color-primary`, `--color-secondary`, titles, and logos).
- **Feature Gating**: Tiered access control (e.g., Pro Plan Advanced Analytics).
